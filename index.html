WASM SIMD Benchmark with Dot Product

This is a simple benchmark that demonstrates the benefits of using SIMD intrinsics when working with paralelizable processes in javascript. The benchmark compares the speed of computing dot product of 2 arrays in 3 different ways: Vanilla JS, vanilla wasm with Rust, and wasm with Rust using SIMD.
